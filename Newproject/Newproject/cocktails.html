Cocktails

Milk and banana

A milk and banana cocktail is a beverage made by blending together milk, ripe bananas, and sometimes other ingredients such as sugar, honey, or vanilla extract.

Milk and chookolate

A milk and chocolate cocktail is a beverage made by blending together milk, chocolate syrup, and sometimes other ingredients such as sugar, vanilla extract, or whipped cream. It is a rich and indulgent drink that is often enjoyed as a dessert or a sweet treat.

Mixed fruit cocktail

A mixed fruit cocktail is a beverage made by blending together a variety of fresh or frozen fruits, along with ingredients such as juice, syrup, or yogurt. It is a colorful and flavorful drink that is often enjoyed as a refreshing snack or a healthy option. Common fruits used in mixed fruit cocktails include strawberries, bananas, pineapples, and mangoes.
